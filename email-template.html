Security 102: Information Asset Classification
It’s time to take the new P&G Security 102 training on Information Asset Classification. You have received an email from Businessconduct.im that contains a link to the training module. All documents must be labeled with their Classification Level and everyone at P&G is responsible for implementing this policy. Whether physical or digital, it is imperative that we all understand how to handle our company’s information in ways that are proportionate to its level of sensitivity and risk of exposure.

Ready to begin? Here's how:
• Take the Security 102 training that was emailed to you.
• Learn the 4 classification levels for P&G assets.
• Make sure the Information Asset Classification toolbar has been installed on your desktop.
• Use the toolbar to start labeling all of the data and documents you work with.
• Store all data and documents according to the specific instructions for their classification level.
Need any help or have more questions? Visit the new IT Policy website at ITPolicy.pg.com.

Get Started
On July 1, 2018, the new Information Asset Classification Policy will go into effect. Visit ITPolicy.pg.com to find everything you need to start implementing the policy.
